What is Mealboard?

We are the first advertising platform that prints ads on takeaway packagings, distributes them to merchants for free, and provides trackable analytics, ensuring your messages reach relaxed and attentive audiences in both out-of-home and in-home settings.

Mealboard brings you the first-ever advertising solution that places your brand directly in front of customers during their meals. This ensures that your ads won't be ignored, skipped, missed, and overlooked.
